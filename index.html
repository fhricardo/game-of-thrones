Before the Winter coming, Theres will be a new King of Westeros

Historically, most of Westeros used to be divided into seven different kingdoms, including all lands south of The Wall in the distant north. After the Targaryen Conquest, the kingdoms were all unified into one realm, but the common name persisted: the title of the monarch on the Iron Throne was "Lord of the Seven Kingdoms". The two names are often used interchangeably, though the lands Beyond the Wall were never actually part of the "seven kingdoms".
